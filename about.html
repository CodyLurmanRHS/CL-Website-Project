About.
Sora Shima ('Sky / Island' in Japanese) is an instrumental Post-Rock band from New Zealand.
Formed in early 2006 under the name Amelia Blackout, the band changed to the current moniker in early 2007.
Sora Shima fuses elements of heavy Indie, Cinematic Ambient and Electronic into something dynamic, immersive and entirely their own.
Their music invokes a profound emotional response in the listener, through careful weaving of melodic, echo-laden Guitar and Powerful rhythms, often underpinned by layers of rich, widescreen Ambience.
